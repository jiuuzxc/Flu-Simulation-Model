Simulation


The model was developed with the intention of providing an accessible, data-driven tool that educates individuals and offers insights into both infection risk and the virus’s behavior in the body with the use of an algorithm “XGBoost” as it was proven for its efficiency and performance in handling with large datasets that allows us to create a predictive model that can offer quick and reliable estimated results for the probability of infections spreading throughout the body.
